Ethan's Mom's Killer Kugel

Description

The cherished kugel recipe that has been passed down through generations in my family is a testament to our cultural heritage and the enduring connection it provides. This rich and indulgent dish, traditionally baked to perfection in a rectangular casserole dish, combines the comforting flavors of egg noodles, sweet raisins, and a creamy blend of sour cream and cream cheese. Generously seasoned with cinnamon and nutmeg, it exudes a warm, inviting aroma that fills the kitchen during preparation. The secret ingredient, a touch of love and tradition, is what truly makes this kugel special. With every silky, baked bite, I am transported to family gatherings of years gone by, savoring the memories of loved ones who shared this recipe and the stories that accompany it. This kugel recipe is not just a dish; it is a cherished link to our past, a connection to our roots, and a reminder of the love and tradition that bind us together as a family. -Chat GPT

Iingredients

  • Butter, for greasing the pan
  • 16 oz. wide egg noodles
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/2 c. butter, melted
  • 16 oz. sour cream
  • 8 oz. cottage cheese
  • 3/4 c. granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Grease a 9" x 13” baking dish.
  3. In a large pot of boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, 5 minutes. Drain.
  4. In a large bowl, mix together eggs, butter, sour cream, cottage cheese, sugar, and cinnamon.
  5. Stir in noodles.
  6. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish.
  7. Bake until set, approximately 1 hour.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil if the top starts to get too dark.
  9. ENJOY!
